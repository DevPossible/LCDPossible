## Details

Tetris-style falling blocks with AI players.

Modes:
- falling-blocks:1 - Single player AI
- falling-blocks:2 - Two player split-screen AI
- falling-blocks:3 - Three player AI

Features:
- Classic tetromino pieces
- AI players with varying skill
- Score tracking
- Line clear animations

Watch AI players compete in the classic puzzle game.
