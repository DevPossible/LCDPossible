## Overview

The LCDPossible falling-blocks panel serves as an innovative screensaver that transforms idle computer monitors into a dynamic Tetris simulation featuring AI gameplay. Catering to single players through multiple modesΓÇösingle, split-screen with two opponents (falling-blocks:2), and three player competition (falling-blocks:3)ΓÇöthis software not only offers entertainment but also enables users of varying skill levels to engage in competitive play.
